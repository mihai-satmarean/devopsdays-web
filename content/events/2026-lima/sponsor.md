<p><strong>DevOpsDays Lima</strong> is fundamentally a community-focused event. Organizations get the most value when they actively participate and genuinely connect with attendees as peers. We recommend bringing technical professionals who can engage with participants in an authentic and meaningful way. Aggressive marketing tactics are generally not well received in this collaborative environment.</p>

<p><strong>Boost your brand at Peru’s leading DevOps event</strong>. DevOpsDays Lima is a conference organized by volunteers dedicated to the DevOps community. The success of this event depends greatly on the support of organizations committed to technological innovation.</p>

<p><strong>Why sponsor DevOpsDays Lima?</strong> By becoming a sponsor, your organization will:<br>
&check; Gain strategic visibility among more than 450 technology professionals, including DevOps engineers, cloud specialists, developers, IT leaders, and decision-makers.<br>
&check; Receive prominent recognition on our website and social media channels before, during, and after the event.<br>
&check; Have the opportunity to deliver short presentations during the official program (depending on the sponsorship level).<br>
&check; Build valuable connections with a growing community in the Peruvian tech market.</p>

<h2>Community Sponsorship</h2>
<p>We offer non-monetary strategic partnerships for like-minded organizations. This option is designed especially for regional conferences, tech communities, and specialized media outlets that want to build mutually beneficial collaborations. To qualify as a community sponsor, your organization should share our vision of driving technological transformation in Lima, commit to actively promoting DevOpsDays Lima through your channels, and follow our {{< event_link page="conduct" text="Code of Conduct" >}}. In return, community sponsors will receive two complimentary tickets to the event, brand placement on our official websites, and visibility in our promotional materials and participant communications.</p>

<h2>Data Policy</h2>
<p>DevOpsDays Lima does not share attendee registration information directly with sponsors. However, sponsors may collect information with participants’ explicit consent during the event, especially through interactions at their booth.</p>
