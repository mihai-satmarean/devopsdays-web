<h2>Airport</h2>
<p>At the airport, you can find the <strong>official taxi counters</strong>. Taxi companies usually offer a standard flat fare from the airport to the LCC of around $25. You may pay a little less or a little more—make sure you travel safely. If you choose ride-hailing services like Uber, make sure to coordinate your pickup clearly.</p>

<br>

<h2>Public Transport</h2>
<p>If you take <strong>Line 1 of the Metro de Lima</strong> (often called “el tren”), you can get off at <strong>La Cultura Station</strong> (Av. Aviación / Av. Javier Prado Este). From there, the LCC is a short walk away.</p>
<p>If you take the <strong>Metropolitano</strong> (BRT) along the Vía Expresa, you can get off at <strong>Canadá</strong> or <strong>Javier Prado</strong> stations. From there, continue toward Av. Aviación by local transport (bus/taxi) and then walk to the venue. Please stay aware of your surroundings.</p>

<br>

<h2>Parking & Personal Mobility</h2>
<p>If you use your own transportation (scooter, bicycle, motorcycle, or car), we recommend checking nearby parking options, such as the parking available at <a href="https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Centro%20Comercial%20La%20Rambla%20San%20Borja%20Lima" target="_blank" rel="noopener">La Rambla shopping mall</a> and the surrounding area.</p>

<br>

<h2>Accessibility</h2>
<p>The venue is fully accessible for people with reduced mobility, including ramps, elevators, and accessible restrooms on all levels. For specific requirements, please contact the {{< event_link page="contact" text="organizers" >}}.</p>
