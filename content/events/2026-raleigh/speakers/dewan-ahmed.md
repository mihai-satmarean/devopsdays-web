as a developer, QA lead, consultant, and developer advocate. For the last fourteen years, Dewan has worked to solve DevOps and infrastructure problems for small startups, large enterprises, and governments. Starting his public speaking at a toastmaster in 2016, 
he has been speaking at tech conferences and meetups for the last nine years. His work is fueled by a passion for open-source and a deep respect for the tech community. Dewan writes about app/data infrastructure, developer advocacy, and his thoughts around a 
career in tech on his personal blog.
