Dan McKinley is a raconteur and engineer. He was in the first 20 employees at Etsy, and worked at Stripe, Mailchimp, and his own startups. 
His talk “Choose Boring Technology” is fairly popular online. He was a VP of Engineering at Mozilla before boomeranging back to Etsy to build stuff and enjoy life again.
